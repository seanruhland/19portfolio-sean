Designer & Developer

For the past year I worked as an apprentice under a senior web developer. I learned a lot Javascript along with React and some of the more modern work practices that one can expect from a startup environment. While there I was able to wear a lot of different hats and really grew as a professional in a tech-type environment. I found that engineering is a great fit for me. I love to travel and explore the world and feel that having these outside experiences has contributed greatly to who I am.
